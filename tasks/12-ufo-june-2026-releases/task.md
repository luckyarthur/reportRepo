# Task: Scrape and Summarize UFO/UAP News & Releases — June 2026

Write the result to `output.md` in this folder.

## Objective
Research the web for **everything newly released or newly disclosed about UFOs / UAP (Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena) during June 2026**, then produce a concise, well-sourced summary of that month's new content.

"New release info" should be interpreted broadly and include any of the following that were **published, released, or first reported in June 2026**:
- Government / military disclosures, hearings, or declassified reports (e.g., U.S. DoD AARO, Congressional hearings, foreign-government releases).
- New official UAP sighting reports, datasets, or statistics published that month.
- Peer-reviewed papers, scientific studies, or institutional reports (e.g., Galileo Project, NASA).
- Notable media releases tied to the topic: documentaries, books, films, TV episodes, podcasts, or major investigative articles.
- Significant credible witness testimony or whistleblower statements first made public in June 2026.

## Steps of work
1. **Search.** Use web search to find UFO/UAP news, reports, and media dated within **June 1–30, 2026**. Run multiple queries (e.g., "UAP June 2026", "AARO report June 2026", "UFO disclosure hearing 2026", "new UAP documentary 2026", "Galileo Project 2026 findings"). Cast a wide net across news, government, and science sources.
2. **Fetch & verify.** Open the most relevant/credible sources and confirm the publication/release date falls in June 2026. Discard anything outside that window or undated.
3. **Deduplicate & rank.** Group coverage of the same event; rank items by significance and source credibility (official government > peer-reviewed > major outlets > blogs/forums).
4. **Summarize.** Write the summary described below.
5. **Cite.** Every claim must carry a source link and the publication date.

## Required structure of `output.md`
- **Title + scope line** stating the coverage window (June 2026) and the date the research was performed.
- **TL;DR** — 3–5 bullet points capturing the most important June 2026 developments.
- **New releases & disclosures (detailed)** — one subsection per item, each with:
  - What it is (report / hearing / paper / film / book / testimony).
  - Who released it and the exact release date.
  - A 2–4 sentence summary of its content and why it matters.
  - Source link(s).
- **Media & entertainment releases** — table of any UFO-themed films, shows, books, documentaries, or podcasts released in June 2026 (Title | Type | Release date | Platform/Publisher | One-line description | Source).
- **Cross-cutting themes** — short paragraph on patterns across the month's content.
- **Sources** — consolidated list of all links used, each tagged with its publication date.

## Rules & cautions
- **Accuracy over completeness.** This is a current-events scraping task — only include items you can verify with a real, dated source. Do **not** fabricate reports, hearings, titles, or dates.
- If web access is unavailable or returns no verifiable June 2026 results, say so explicitly in `output.md`, list the exact queries attempted, and report "No verifiable June 2026 releases found" rather than inventing content.
- Clearly separate **official/scientific** items from **speculative/tabloid** items; label credibility.
- Note any major claim that is contested or unconfirmed as such.

## Style
- Neutral, journalistic tone — no sensationalism.
- ~600–1000 words plus the media table and sources list.
- Prefer primary sources (government, journals) over aggregators where possible.
